DESIGN SYSTEM · DARK MODE FIRST

A toolkit for high‑density product software.

Halogen is a dark-mode design system for capability-dense products — dashboards, workspace tools, data platforms, anything where lots of capability lives on one screen. Built around a near-black surface stack, two saturated accents (lime & magenta) and a pixel-terminal display face.

How do credits work?

Credits are the unit of work. Each task spends a different amount based on its size and duration. Plan credits refresh monthly and don't roll over; bonus credits expire when the plan ends.

Is my subscription auto-renewed?

Yes — you can pause or cancel anytime from Settings → Billing. We never charge without a renewal notice 7 days in advance.

How many tasks can I run?

Each tier shows an estimated count. The exact number depends on the size, duration, and module you pick.

Can I change my subscription after purchase?

Upgrade any time and pay the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Usage Rules

do · don't
DO
  • Use one primary accent per screen. Lime for the main CTA, magenta only for time-bound promos.
  • Pair pixel display type with neutral body — let the headline carry the personality.
  • Anchor every card to a token: --ink-100 for resting, --ink-150 for inset.
  • Use the 4px spacing scale exclusively. Half-steps break vertical rhythm.
DON'T
  • Don't put lime & magenta CTAs side-by-side — they compete and visual hierarchy collapses.
  • Don't introduce a new hue. The accent set is closed.
  • Don't set body copy in the pixel display face — it's a hero-only typeface.
  • Don't use pure black (#000). The page floor is --ink-050.
